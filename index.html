Educational Tour — Cebu & Bohol

Day 1 — UP Cebu

On the first day, visit UP Cebu, a premier institution for IT studies. Explore the campus, interact with faculty and students, and gain insight into the university’s innovative IT programs, research, and technology-driven academic initiatives.

Day 2 — Rivant IT Cebu

On Day 2, visit Rivant IT Cebu, a cutting-edge tech company that showcases the latest developments in IT, software solutions, and innovation. Participate in a hands-on workshop focusing on the tech industry and its applications in the real world.

Day 3 — Mata Technologies

Head to Bohol to visit Mata Technologies, a leader in IT and sustainable technology solutions. Learn about cutting-edge developments in software, IoT, and green tech, and participate in a workshop on how technology can address environmental challenges.

Day 4 — T.A.R.S.I.E.R 117

Conclude your tour at T.A.R.S.I.E.R 117, an initiative focused on technology for social good. Participate in a community-driven workshop on IT solutions for sustainable tourism and community development, and discuss the potential of IT in solving global challenges.
